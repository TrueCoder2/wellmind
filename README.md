# 🧠 MindMate

> An AI-powered student wellness companion that helps students monitor their mental well-being, reflect on their emotions, identify stress patterns, and receive personalized support throughout their academic journey.

## Overview

Preparing for competitive exams and board examinations can be mentally exhausting. Students often face anxiety, burnout, self-doubt, lack of motivation, and result-related stress. MindMate was built to provide a safe and supportive space where students can check in with their emotions, understand their mental health trends, and receive actionable wellness guidance powered by AI.

The platform combines mood tracking, journaling, analytics, and personalized AI insights to help students build healthier study habits and maintain emotional balance during high-pressure academic periods.

## Why MindMate?

Academic success should not come at the cost of mental well-being.

MindMate empowers students to become more aware of their emotional state, recognize unhealthy patterns early, and develop sustainable habits that support both academic performance and personal wellness.

Rather than focusing solely on productivity, the platform encourages a healthier and more balanced approach to learning.

---

## How It Works

1. Complete a daily wellness check-in.
2. Record your mood, stress levels, sleep quality, and study activity.
3. Reflect on your thoughts through journaling.
4. Receive AI-generated wellness insights and recommendations.
5. Track your emotional trends and progress over time.
6. Build healthier study and wellness habits.

## Disclaimer

MindMate is designed for wellness support and self-reflection purposes only.

This application does not provide medical advice, diagnose mental health conditions, or replace professional counseling services. Students experiencing severe emotional distress should seek support from trusted guardians, educators, counselors, or qualified mental health professionals.
